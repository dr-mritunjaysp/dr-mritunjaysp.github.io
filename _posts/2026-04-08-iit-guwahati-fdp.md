Completed a **one-week Faculty Development Programme (FDP)** organized by the **E&ICT Academy, IIT Guwahati** in association with **UPES Dehradun**.

### Programme Details

- **Title:** Advanced Architectures and Real-Time Systems for Intelligent Embedded Applications
- **Duration:** January 05-09, 2026
- **Organizer:** E&ICT Academy, Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati
- **Associated Institute:** University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), Dehradun
- **Reference No.:** EICTP-ITOF/25-26/009010

### Highlights

This FDP focused on advanced computing architectures and real-time systems for intelligent embedded applications. The programme aligned well with my academic interests in intelligent systems, embedded computing, Blockchain, IoT, Edge AI, and next-generation research-driven teaching.

It also contributed to my continued professional development as **Assistant Professor (Selection Grade) at UPES Dehradun**, strengthening my engagement with emerging technologies and applied interdisciplinary research.
